The Rules:

There are 16 cards, each are a pair. You must choose the same pairs to WIN the game.

When you turn over two cards that do not match, those cards are turned face down again.

There will be a limited amount of time for you to find the cards.

If you do not match the gards within that given time, you LOSE!

There is also one catch, if the number of flips is greater than 40 you will lose.

Choose wisely to win and use you MEMORY to the fullest.

GOOD LUCK!
